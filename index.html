Frequently Asked Questions

What are the clinic timings?

Weekdays and Saturday: 9:00 AM – 9:00 PM. Sunday: 9:00 AM – 1:00 PM.

Is root canal treatment painful?

No — with modern rotary endodontics and effective anaesthesia, RCT at our clinic is essentially painless and usually completed in 1–2 visits.

Do you treat children?

Yes, Dr Agrawal provides gentle pediatric dental care for children of all ages in a child-friendly environment.

How do I book an appointment?

We follow strict sterilization protocols — every instrument and surface is thoroughly sterilized before each patient appointment.
